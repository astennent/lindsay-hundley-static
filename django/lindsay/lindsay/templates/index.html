About

Lindsay Hundley is a graduate student in the Department of Political Science at Stanford University. Prior to pursuing her Ph.D., she was the project manager for the Teaching, Research, and International Policy (TRIP) Project and a research fellow for the Project on International Peace and Security (PIPS) at the College of William and Mary.

Lindsay’s primary fields of study are international relations—in particular, international security—and formal theory. Her substantive research interests include rhetoric in crisis bargaining, elite discourse on foreign policy issues, and alliances and coalitions during war. She also does work on the application of automated text methods to classical political texts, such as Thucydides’ History of the Peloponnesian War.
